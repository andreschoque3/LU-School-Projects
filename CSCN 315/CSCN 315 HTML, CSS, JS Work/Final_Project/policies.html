Library Policies

    List of Major Policies

    I. Operational Hours

    The Library opens from 8:30 am until 5 pm. We do not accept any books that have been checked out after 5 or earlier than 8:30. We accept anyone who has a valid library card. Library cards can be purchased at the front desk. anyone without a card will not be permitted.

    II. Library Card

    Library cards act as your 'badge' and ID for the library. This helps us identify who is using our services and it helps identify who are guests and non members. Guest cards can be obtained at the front desk.

    III. Security

    These are the following security measures we have implemented to keep everyone safe: cameras at every corner, multiple security guards around each study area and room. And surpervision from the front desk.

    IV. Emergency info

    For your safety we are always making sure that everyone is safe. That is why we implemented security measures such as cards and passes. We can to make sure there are no infiltrators and if they are we can monitor and take care of business. If there are any suspicious activity please notify the front desk immediately.

    V. Book Policy

    To checkout a book simply grab any book that you would like to check out. Once you have your book of your liking, go to the front office to check it out. You follow the directions that the front desk administrator tells you and then you should be good to go. Checked books have a 2 month timeline. You should return it afeter those two months or we will charge every day after the date checked out. The fee increases 5 dollars every day. If you would like to pruchase the book please do it online. Unless otherwise mentioned. You can take up to 8 books with you. You can check out online on the checkout portal.

    VI. Study policy

    Please be aware of your surroundings and be mindful of other people. Please do not be a distraction towards other people. People come in to take a break and read, or to work diligently on their work. Please do not cause any problems with your fellow neighbors or you will be asked to leave the library. Study and reading spaces are meant for only that.

    VII. Reserving Study Rooms

    Reserving study rooms can be done online or at the front desk. Always state why you need to reserve a study room. This is due to high demand. If we catch you doing other things we will kick you out of the room and give it to the next person.

    VIII. Reserving Conference Rooms

    The procedure to reserve a conference room is through the online portal. Conference rooms cannot be reserved at the front desk since they need anticipation. Please check our available time slots.

    IX. Food and Drinks

    The Library will not tolerate any food or drinks being consumed at any room. Please refrain from eating since it causes distractions and it is hard on the custodians.

    X. Noise at the Library

    People at any room should have an inside voice. The only exemptions are the study and conference rooms. But we would also politely ask to monitor your voice levels. If you speak very loudly or make intruptions you will be kicked out.
